Pasta Salad

Pasta salad is one of the easiest and most delicious meals to make. Cook a pasta of your choice and add your favorite vegetables, cheese or meats covered with a homemade dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of dried pasta (penne, rotini, fusilli)
  • 1 1/2 cups of cherry tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 can of sliced black olives
  • 1 can of artichoke hearts, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 200g of feta cheese, crumbled

    • Dressing:

  • 1/3 cup of white wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp of dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 cups of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp celery salt
  • 1 tbsp of lemon juice
  • salt and pepper to taste

Method:

  1. Bring pot of salted water to boil. Cook pasta as directed. Drain pasta and run under cold water until noodles are cool.
  2. While pasta cooks, prep the vegetables and cheese. Chop into desired bite sized pieces. Empty canned ingredients and chop. Throw all prepped ingredients into large mixing bowl.
  3. Make the dressing. Add all of the dresing ingredients together and mix thoroughly. Taste and adjust to desired flavor.
  4. Enjoy and eat the fruits of your labor!
